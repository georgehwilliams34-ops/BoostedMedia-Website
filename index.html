FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.

How fast can we start?

Usually within 3–7 days. If you need a quick turnaround, we can often start even sooner depending on availability.

Do you film on-site?

Yes — we come to you. We film in batches so you get weeks of content from one session.

Do I have to be in the videos?

No — you don’t have to be in any of the videos. However, we highly encourage it because it usually boosts engagement, views, and sales.

Do you post to all platforms?

Yes. TikTok, Instagram Reels, Facebook — we tailor the captions and structure for each.

Is there a contract?

No long contracts. You can cancel anytime. We focus on earning the results every month.
