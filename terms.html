Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe. It applies to boostedmedia.uk and any services we provide to our clients.

Who we are

Boostedmedia — Scunthorpe, UK. You can contact us at boostedmediabusiness@gmail.com.

What data we collect

  • Contact details (name, email, phone) when you submit a form or book a call.
  • Business information you share with us during discovery and onboarding.
  • Website analytics (aggregated, non-identifying) to improve our site.

How we use your data

  • To reply to enquiries and provide quotes.
  • To deliver our services and manage your account.
  • To send essential service messages. We’ll only send marketing with your consent and you can opt out anytime.

Lawful bases

We process personal data on the bases of contract (to deliver services), consent (for marketing), and legitimate interests (site security, improvement).

Sharing & transfers

We don’t sell your data. We may share it with trusted processors (e.g., email/hosting providers) under data-processing agreements. Data may be processed outside the UK/EU with appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs).

Cookies

We use essential cookies and minimal analytics. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purpose collected or to meet legal/financial obligations, then delete or anonymise it.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your data, and you can object to processing. You also have the right to complain to the ICO.
