Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Last updated: 7 September 2025

Summary Who we are Data we collect How we use data Lawful bases Sharing Cookies Retention Your rights Contact Changes

1) Summary

We collect only what we need to deliver and improve our services (e.g., respond to enquiries, schedule calls, and manage projects). We never sell your data.

2) Who we are

Boostedmedia (“we”, “us”) — Scunthorpe, UK.
Email: boostedmediabusiness@gmail.com

3) Data we collect

  • Contact data — name, email, phone, business details submitted via forms.
  • Usage data — basic analytics (page views, device, referrer) to improve the website.
  • Client content — assets you supply for projects (text, images, video).

4) How we use data

  • Respond to enquiries and deliver our services.
  • Schedule calls and manage projects.
  • Send service updates and essential notices.
  • Improve our website and offerings.

5) Lawful bases (UK GDPR)

  • Contract — to provide the services you request.
  • Legitimate interests — to run and improve our business in ways that do not override your rights.
  • Consent — where required (e.g., certain marketing). You can withdraw consent at any time.

6) Sharing

We may share data with trusted processors (e.g., hosting, analytics, customer support tools) under data-processing agreements. We do not sell your data.

7) Cookies & analytics

We use essential cookies and minimal analytics to understand site usage. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

8) Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined above or to comply with legal obligations, then delete or anonymise it.

9) Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can request: access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, and objection to processing. You may also complain to the ICO.

10) Contact

Email us at boostedmediabusiness@gmail.com for any privacy request.

11) Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We’ll post the new version here and update the “Last updated” date.
